# Hook 黄金三秒钩子框架

> 前 3 秒决定视频生死。本文件提供 12 种经过验证的 Hook 框架，附跨品类示例和选择指南。

---

## Hook 选择速查

| 你的产品特点 | 推荐 Hook 类型 |
|---|---|
| 效果有强烈视觉冲击 | Pattern Interrupt, Before/After Bridge |
| 替代昂贵方案 | Myth Buster, Authority Builder |
| 解决常见痛点 | Problem Agitate Solution, Quick Win |
| 小众/新奇产品 | Hidden Secret, Curiosity Gap |
| 有数据/评价支撑 | Social Proof, Authority Builder |
| 需要制造紧迫感 | FOMO, Negative Hook |
| 针对特定人群 | Identity Hook, Educational Hook |

---

## 经典 Hook 框架（10 种）

### 1. Pattern Interrupt（模式打断）
**原理**: 用反常识的画面或言论打断用户"无意识滑屏"的惯性

**框架**: 惊人视觉/声明 → 悬念 → 引入方案 → 证据

**跨品类示例**:
- 🔪 厨房: *"Learning how to sharpen a knife is a WASTE of time"* — 直接否定常识
- 📱 3C: *"I threw my $300 case in the trash after finding this $15 one"* — 反价格直觉
- 💄 美妆: *"Your skincare routine is actually DESTROYING your skin"* — 攻击现有习惯

---

### 2. Problem Agitate Solution（痛点放大解决）
**原理**: 先触发痛感，再放大焦虑，最后给出救赎

**框架**: 指出问题 → 放大影响 → 呈现方案 → 展示转变

**跨品类示例**:
- 🏠 家居: *"Your kitchen sponge has more bacteria than your toilet — here's what I switched to"*
- 🏋️ 健身: *"I've been working out for 2 years and saw ZERO results... until I changed this one thing"*
- 🚗 汽配: *"That weird noise your car makes? It's costing you $500 every month you ignore it"*

---

### 3. Hidden Secret（隐藏秘密）
**原理**: 暗示有一个"大多数人不知道"的信息，制造信息不对称焦虑

**框架**: 暗示发现 → 激发好奇 → 揭露方法 → 说明应用

**跨品类示例**:
- 🧹 清洁: *"The cleaning hack hotels don't want you to know about"*
- 💰 省钱: *"There's a secret Amazon page with 90% off items and nobody talks about it"*
- 🍳 厨房: *"Chefs have been hiding this $12 tool from us for years"*

---

### 4. Before/After Bridge（前后对比桥）
**原理**: 视觉对比产生认知冲突，对比越极端冲击越大

**框架**: 展示"之前" → 暗示转变 → 连接方案 → 验证"之后"

**跨品类示例**:
- 🔧 工具: *"This rusty blade → razor sharp in 30 seconds"* (配画面对比)
- 💅 美甲: *"My nails were WRECKED... 2 weeks later they look like this"*
- 🏡 家居: *"My bathroom looked like a crime scene, now look at it"*

---

### 5. Authority Builder（权威建立）
**原理**: 用专业身份或数据颠覆大众认知

**框架**: 提出流行观点 → 用数据反驳 → 展示更优方案 → 成果佐证

**跨品类示例**:
- 🦷 个护: *"As a dentist, I'm begging you to stop using [popular product]"*
- 🍖 厨房: *"I'm a chef and I've NEVER used an expensive knife — here's why"*
- 💊 保健: *"Fun fact: 90% of the supplements you're taking do literally nothing"*

---

### 6. Educational Hook（教育型）
**原理**: 许诺即时获得有价值的知识

**框架**: 提出引人思考的问题 → 给出有价值信息 → 释放方案 → 鼓励行动

**跨品类示例**:
- 🔋 3C: *"3 things your phone can do that Apple doesn't tell you about"*
- 🧴 美妆: *"The right order to apply your skincare — 90% of people get it wrong"*
- 🏠 家居: *"5 things making your house smell weird and you don't even notice"*

---

### 7. Social Proof（社会认同）
**原理**: "很多人都在用"触发从众心理

**框架**: 展示群体行为/数据 → 建立可信度 → 解释方法 → 引导下一步

**跨品类示例**:
- 🛒 通用: *"OK so 2 million people bought this thing and I finally get why"*
- 💄 美妆: *"This has 50k 5-star reviews and honestly I'm obsessed"*
- 🏋️ 健身: *"My entire gym uses this and I was the last to find out"*

---

### 8. Myth Buster（打破迷思）
**原理**: 推翻用户现有认知，制造"必须更新知识"的冲动

**框架**: 引入常见认知 → 推翻 → 呈现真相 → 用证据支撑

**跨品类示例**:
- 🧴 护肤: *"Expensive skincare is a SCAM — here's what actually works for $8"*
- 🔪 厨房: *"You do NOT need a $200 knife set, and I can prove it"*
- 🏋️ 健身: *"Cardio doesn't burn fat — this does"*

---

### 9. Quick Win（快速获胜）
**原理**: 承诺即时可得的结果，降低行动门槛

**框架**: 承诺即时就效 → 描述简单方法 → 强调易操作性 → 确认快速性

**跨品类示例**:
- 🧹 清洁: *"Make your entire bathroom spotless in 60 seconds with this"*
- 💇 个护: *"Fix your messy hair in 10 seconds, I'm not joking"*
- 📱 3C: *"Double your phone battery life with this one setting change"*

---

### 10. FOMO（害怕错过）
**原理**: 利用"再不行动就来不及了"的焦虑

**框架**: 呈现机会 → 制造紧迫 → 描述错过的损失 → 提供方案

**跨品类示例**:
- 🛍️ 促销: *"This is going viral and it's about to sell out again"*
- 🏠 家居: *"I found this at TJ Maxx for $12 and it's a $60 product everywhere else"*
- 🎮 3C: *"If you haven't gotten this yet, you're literally sleeping on it"*

---

## 新型 Hook（2026 趋势，2 种）

### 11. Negative Hook（否定式钩子）🆕
**原理**: 逆反心理——"让我看看你为什么不推荐"，同时暗示有隐藏条件

**框架**: 否定式开场 → 制造反转预期 → 揭示条件 → 展示真实价值

**跨品类示例**:
- 🔪 厨房: *"Do NOT buy this knife sharpener... unless you actually want sharp knives"*
- 💄 美妆: *"I can't recommend this foundation... for anyone with oily skin. Dry skin? GAME CHANGER"*
- 🏋️ 健身: *"Don't waste your money on this... unless you're serious about gains"*

---

### 12. Identity Hook（身份认同钩子）🆕
**原理**: 精准圈定目标人群，让"不是目标"的人划走，让"是目标"的人产生强烈归属感

**框架**: 精准身份标签 → 引发共鸣 → 连接产品 → 强化归属

**跨品类示例**:
- 🏢 职场: *"If you work from home and your back is KILLING you, stop scrolling"*
- 🐕 宠物: *"Dog parents who are tired of fur on EVERYTHING — this is for you"*
- 👶 育儿: *"Moms who are running on 3 hours of sleep, I found something that actually helps"*

## Hook 组合公式

> 最强 Hook = **视觉冲击** + **文字叠加** + **口播钩子** 三层同时出击

| 层级 | 作用 | 示例 |
|---|---|---|
| 画面 | 抓住眼球（3 秒内必须有运动/冲突/反差） | 刀切纸的慢镜头、脏→干净的对比 |
| 文字 | 捕获静音用户（红色/黄色大字，关键词全大写） | "WASTE" "SCAM" "GAME CHANGER" |
| 口播 | 传递语气和情绪（惊讶/愤怒/兴奋） | 语速加快 + 语气上扬 |
